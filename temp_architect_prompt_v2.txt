    async def event_generator():

What could be the reason the UI timeline isn't moving past Phase 4? The reason the dashboard is stuck at Phase 4 is because the `if/else` logic in `get_current_phase()` evaluates from top to bottom. Once `os.path.exists(os.path.join(latest_run, 'outputs'))` is true, the function returns immediately, effectively "short-circuiting" the check for higher-numbered phases (like 5, 6, or 7) that might have been reached later. To fix this, you should order your checks from the highest phase number to the lowest.
